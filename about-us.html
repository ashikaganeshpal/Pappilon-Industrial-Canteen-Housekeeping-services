About Us

Pappilon has been a trusted name in industrial catering and housekeeping services since 1990. Our vision is to provide hygienic, healthy food and excellent housekeeping services through innovative, cost-effective solutions.

With over three decades of experience, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for major industrial and institutional clients across India. Our services incorporate the latest technologies and methods in food production, storage, service maintenance, and nutritional values while maintaining strict hygiene standards.
